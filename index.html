Top Freedom Fighters

Mahatma Gandhi

Mahatma Gandhi led India’s non-violent independence movement against British rule. His principles of non-violence and civil disobedience inspired global movements for civil rights.

Jawaharlal Nehru

Jawaharlal Nehru was India’s first Prime Minister. His vision for a modern and progressive India laid the foundation for India’s industrial and technological growth.

Narendra Damodardas Modi

Our current Prime Minister is Narendra Damodardas Modi. He has been leading the country with a vision of progress and development, focusing on economic growth, digital innovation, and social justice.
Narendra Damodardas Modi is the current Prime Minister of India, serving since May 2014. Born on September 17, 1950, in Vadnagar, Gujarat, he was previously the Chief Minister of Gujarat from 2001 to 2014. Modi is a member of the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) and the Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS). His tenure as Prime Minister has focused on economic reforms, digital infrastructure, and social initiatives like Swachh Bharat (Clean India). He is known for his strong leadership style and efforts to boost India's global standing. Modi was re-elected for a second term in 2019, emphasizing development and national security.

Cultural Events

Independence Day is celebrated with a range of cultural events including flag hoisting ceremonies, patriotic performances, and traditional dances. Local communities host parades and educational programs to honor the nation's history.

  • Flag Hoisting Ceremony at the Red Fort
  • Patriotic Songs and Dance Performances.
  • Local Parades and Community Gatherings.
